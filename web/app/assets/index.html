Spring Gala 2019

Details

Join us for our members-only Spring Gala, happening at the AMS Nest Saturday, March 23rd, from 18:00 - 21:00. The event will feature a range of performances by talented members of the club. Food made by our executives and volunteers will also be available for order.

RSVPs require a $5.00 deposit. The money can be used as credit during the event. Due to Stripe fees, however, online RSVPs will require a $0.50 surcharge. Apologies for the inconvenience.

We're still looking for performances and volunteers. If you'd like to have a chance to show off, please fill out the performances registration form. Similarly, if you'd like to volunteer, please fill out the volunteer form.

More details of our event, as well as updates at a later time, can be found on our Facebook event page.
